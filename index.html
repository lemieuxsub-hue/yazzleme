Meet Tracey

the heart behind YazzleMe

YazzleMe was born from a simple belief: that what you wear can change how you feel — and how you make others feel. Tracey created this brand as a love letter to kindness, positivity, and the power of a great hat.

Every hat in our collection carries that energy. From the softest beanies that feel like a warm hug to statement pieces that spark conversations, YazzleMe is about connection, community, and a whole lot of heart.

Based in Texas with a spirit that reaches everywhere, YazzleMe is proof that a little kindness — and a great hat — can go a long way.
